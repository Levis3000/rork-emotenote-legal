# SixtySec Legal Pages

Static HTML pages for Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

## Files

- `privacy-policy.html` - Privacy Policy
- `terms-of-service.html` - Terms of Service

## Deployment

This repository is configured to deploy to Railway as a static site.

## URLs

Once deployed to Railway:
- Privacy Policy: `https://[your-railway-domain]/privacy-policy.html`
- Terms of Service: `https://[your-railway-domain]/terms-of-service.html`
