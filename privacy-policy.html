Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to SixtySec. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our journaling application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password
  • Journal Entries: Your mood data, journal text, tags, and media (photos, videos, audio)
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Sync your data across devices
  • Generate AI insights and recommendations
  • Improve our app and user experience
  • Send important service notifications

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. Journal entries and media are stored in secure cloud storage with strict access controls. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information.

5. AI Processing

We use artificial intelligence to generate insights and stories from your journal entries. Your journal content is processed through secure AI services but is never used to train AI models or shared with third parties beyond the AI service provider.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information with:

  • Service providers who help us operate the app (cloud storage, authentication)
  • AI service providers for generating insights (with appropriate safeguards)
  • Law enforcement if required by law

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your journal data
  • Opt out of AI features

8. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all your personal data and journal entries are permanently deleted within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Feedback section in the app or via email at privacy@sixtysec.app

By using SixtySec, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
